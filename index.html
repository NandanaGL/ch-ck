Classic Chocolate Cake

Indulge in the ultimate chocolatey delight with this rich and moist chocolate cake recipe. Perfect for any occasion, from birthdays to casual gatherings, this cake is sure to satisfy your sweet cravings. Made with simple ingredients like cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, and flour, it’s a classic that never goes out of style. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a beginner, this chocolate cake is easy to make and guaranteed to impress. Let’s dive into the recipe and make a dessert that everyone will love!

Ingredients

i.Cake:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup whole milk, at room temperature
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

ii.Chocolate Frosting:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons milk or heavy cream

Preparation

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease two 9-inch (23cm) round cake pans.
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a medium bowl.
  3. Whisk cocoa powder and milk in a large bowl. Add eggs one at a time, whisking well after each addition. Whisk in vanilla.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to the chocolate mixture, whisking until smooth.
  5. Divide batter evenly between prepared pans and smooth tops.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Beat butter, confectioners' sugar, cocoa powder and milk until smooth and creamy for the frosting.
  8. Place one cake layer on a serving plate, spread frosting on top, then place the second layer.
  9. Frost the top and sides of the cake with remaining frosting.

Tips:

  • Use high-quality cocoa powder for the best flavor.
  • Don't overmix the batter.
  • Let the cake cool completely before frosting.
  • You can decorate with chocolate shavings or chopped nuts, if desired.
